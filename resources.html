Design Services

Our Core Services:
Project Inception: We begin by collaborating with you to understand your unique vision and requirements. Through open discussions and careful planning, we'll establish a clear project brief that lays the groundwork for your dream space.
Programming & Schematic Design: Our team translates your needs into a functional and creative design proposal. We explore various options, present you with initial sketches and 3D models, and refine the concept until it perfectly reflects your vision.
Design Development & Construction Documents: We meticulously develop your chosen design, creating detailed drawings, including floor plans, sections, elevations, and comprehensive specifications for every building element. These documents ensure clear communication with contractors and serve as the blueprint for your project.
Construction Administration: Your project doesn't end with the drawings. We provide ongoing support throughout construction, visiting the site regularly to ensure the work adheres to the plans and maintains the design intent. We'll also address any questions or concerns that arise during construction, safeguarding your investment.

Dodds Estudio

This architecture, prevalent in the city, was characterized by solving a set of housing units that share the same land and access. Each one gained natural ventilation and lighting through internal patios while fragmented spaces defined its rooms. The design proposal aims to rethink these characteristics as an opportunity to create a central space that would improve the versatility of the environment.
